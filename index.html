🌌 concept & atmosphere

We exist between floors - in an endless foyer with marble floors, soft fluorescent lighting, and music that seems to come from nowhere.
The interior changes depending on the guest's inner state, but some things remain constant:

  • a dark oak counter with golden monograms
  • old maps of non-existent cities on the walls
  • and a menu that always suggests the right choice
"Change Of Scene is not just a coffee shop. It's an assembly point between what was and what could have been".
- from LegitPlayer's diary
🎵🎵 THE FOUNDER - LEGITPLAYER♪

Change Of Scene was founded by a mysterious musician known only as LegitPlayer. Little is known about him - some say he appeared from nowhere, others believe he was always there, playing piano in the space between moments. His music still drifts through the café's speakers, shaping its atmosphere and guiding lost souls home.

LegitPlayer discovered this liminal space in the late 1990s, sensing it through the resonance of a melody he was composing. He placed the first chair, brewed the first coffee, and opened the door for those who needed to find their way. To this day, he visits the café, often late at night, playing melodies that shift with the mood of the room.

"I don't create the music. I just listen to what's already there, between the silence." - LegitPlayer
